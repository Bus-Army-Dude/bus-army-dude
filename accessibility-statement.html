Accessibility Statement

Last updated: February 10, 2025

Commitment to Accessibility

We are committed to making our website accessible to everyone, regardless of ability or technology. We strive to ensure that our website meets accessibility standards and provides an inclusive experience for all users.

Measures to Support Accessibility

We take the following measures to ensure accessibility on our website:

  • Regularly testing and auditing the website for accessibility compliance.
  • Ensuring text alternatives for non-text content (e.g., images).
  • Providing keyboard navigation and interaction for all website elements.
  • Offering text resizing options for better readability.

Conformance Status

Our website is designed to be compliant with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. We aim to maintain this standard as part of our ongoing commitment to accessibility.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us through our website's contact form. We will make every reasonable effort to address your concerns.

Accessibility Support

If you need assistance navigating our website or accessing any content, please reach out to us. We are here to help and will work with you to provide the information you need in an accessible format.

Third-Party Content

Some parts of our website may include third-party content or links. We are not responsible for the accessibility of third-party content, but we encourage these providers to offer accessible services.
